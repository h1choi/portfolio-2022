5th House

Fifth House was founded by Guy Arlington to share his passion for tea and to commemorate his late grandfather, Francois Arlington. Guy fell in love with the rich world of tea as he sampled many cultures and drinks offered by the countries he visited with his grandfather. Francois Arlington was a free-spirited author, who had generational wealth to support his artistic endeavors, which included traveling around the world for his manuscripts. Once Francois fell ill and was unable to travel, Guy crafted a special tea blend to console him. He mixed ingredients that evoked nostalgia of their travels and added a bit of CBD for pain relief. Francois passed away before he tasted the drink he inspired, but it paved the way for his legacy, which is the Fifth House Tea Company (fictional brand).
